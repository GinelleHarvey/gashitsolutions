A7 — Drag & Drop (Cards)

Solitaire-style board

How to play
  • Goal: build each foundation (Spades, Hearts, Diamonds, Clubs) from Ace → King.
  • Drag & drop: move cards between the 7 Tableau piles. Drop on the top half of a card to insert before, bottom half to insert after.
  • Double-click a card to auto-send it to its foundation if it’s the next legal card for that suit.
  • Auto-move All Possible will repeatedly send any cards that can go to a foundation.
  • You can still use the Deck and Discard as scratch areas.
